Self-Care Daily Plan

Handy Checklist For Living & Loving Your Best You

“You yourself, as much as anybody in the entire universe, deserve your love and affection” – Buddha

Happiness Checklist: 45 Minutes A Day To A Happier You



Start the day with visualization

Say affirmations that combat sloppy thinking

Take inspired action that propels you forward

Focus on your breath to stay in the present moment

Do one thing that makes your soul sing

Perform an act of kindness

Let go of one greivance a day

Avoid unproductive words that cause harm

Smile often

Finish the day with gratitude

Checklist inspired by Viva La Me.

Coded by Melanie Bernal.
